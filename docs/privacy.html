Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Nova Focus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

  • School email address — used to create your account and verify student status. We do not share this with third parties.
  • App usage statistics — collected on-device only (Android: via UsageStats API; iOS: estimated). This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Block settings — which apps you choose to block and your schedule. Stored locally on your device.

2. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not share your information with third parties except as required by law.

3. Authentication

We use Supabase to manage authentication. When you sign in, a one-time code is sent to your school email. Supabase stores your email and session token. See Supabase's privacy policy for details.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained until you request deletion. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

5. Children

Nova Focus is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
