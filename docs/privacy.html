Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Student Focus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. What We Collect

  • School email address - used to create your account and verify that you are a student. We do not sell or share this with third parties.
  • App usage statistics - collected and stored on your device only (Android: via the UsageStats API; iOS: estimated from app activity). This data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Block settings - the apps you choose to block and your schedule. Stored locally on your device.
  • Authentication session - a short-lived session token used to keep you signed in, stored securely on your device via encrypted storage.

2. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.
  • We do not store your app usage data on our servers.

3. Authentication

We use Supabase to handle authentication. When you sign in, a one-time verification code is sent to your school email. Supabase stores your email address and session token on their servers. For details, see Supabase's privacy policy.

4. Data Security

Your session token is stored using your device's encrypted secure storage. We use industry-standard practices to protect the data we do hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Your account data (email and session) is retained until you request deletion. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

6. Children

Student Focus is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

Questions about your data or requests for deletion? Email us at livefonam@gmail.com
